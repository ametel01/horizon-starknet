## Contents

The whitepaper (102 pages) covers:

### Main Chapters

1. **Motivation & Problem Statement** — Why yield tokenization matters
2. **System Overview** — Token architecture and contract structure
3. **Formal Model & Notation** — Mathematical definitions and conventions
4. **Core Mechanism Design** — Detailed protocol specifications with pseudocode
5. **Economic Analysis & Incentives** — Market dynamics and participant incentives
6. **Risk Analysis & Threat Model** — Comprehensive security assessment
7. **Governance & Upgrade Model** — Admin powers and upgrade procedures
8. **Related Work** — Comparison with Pendle, Element, and fixed-rate protocols
9. **Conclusion** — Summary and specification status

### Appendices

- **A: Mathematical Proofs** — PT convergence, conservation invariant, no-arbitrage
- **B: Numerical Examples** — Worked examples for PT, LP, and YT flows
- **C: Contract Interfaces** — Complete API specifications
- **D: Glossary** — Term definitions and symbol reference
