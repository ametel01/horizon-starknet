# What is Horizon?

Horizon lets you **lock in guaranteed yields** or **trade future yield** on Starknet.

---

## The Problem

Yield-bearing tokens like nstSTRK have **variable rates** that change daily. You never know what you'll actually earn.

## The Solution

Horizon splits your deposit into two tokens:

  <TableHeader>
    <TableRow>
      <TableHead>Token</TableHead>

    </TableRow>
  </TableHeader>
  <TableBody>
    <TableRow>
      <TableCell>**Principal Token (PT)**</TableCell>
      <TableCell>Fixed yield, guaranteed at maturity</TableCell>
    </TableRow>
    <TableRow>
      <TableCell>**Yield Token (YT)**</TableCell>
      <TableCell>Variable yield until maturity</TableCell>

</Table>

---

## Three Ways to Use Horizon

### 1. Lock in Fixed Yield
Buy PT at a discount. Hold until maturity. Redeem for full value. [Learn more →](/docs/guides/earn-fixed-yield)

**Example:** Pay 95 STRK for PT worth 100 STRK at maturity = 5.26% guaranteed return.

### 2. Trade Yield
Think rates will rise? Buy YT. Think they'll fall? Sell YT. [Learn more →](/docs/guides/trade-yield)

### 3. Earn Fees
Provide liquidity and earn trading fees from other users. [Learn more →](/docs/guides/provide-liquidity)
