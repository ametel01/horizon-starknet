## Implied APY Formula

Derived from PT price and time to maturity:

<Formula display>{"\\text{Implied APY} = \\left( \\frac{1}{P_{PT}} \\right)^{\\frac{365}{d}} - 1"}</Formula>

Where:
- <Formula>{"P_{PT}"}</Formula> = PT price in SY terms
- <Formula>{"d"}</Formula> = days to expiry

## Fixed APY (PT Holders)

When you buy PT at a discount, your fixed APY is:

<Formula display>{"\\text{Fixed APY} = \\left( \\frac{1}{P_{purchase}} \\right)^{\\frac{365}{d}} - 1"}</Formula>

<Callout type="tip">
Your fixed APY is locked in at purchase. It doesn't change regardless of market movements.
</Callout>

## LP APY Components

Liquidity provider returns come from multiple sources:

<Formula display>{"\\text{LP APY} = \\text{Fee APY} + \\text{Underlying Yield} + \\text{PT Appreciation} - \\text{IL}"}</Formula>

Horizon displays **APY** (Annual Percentage Yield) which assumes continuous compounding.

---

## Important Notes

<Callout type="warning">
- **Historical yield ≠ future yield** — Past performance doesn't guarantee future returns
- **Implied yield is market opinion** — It reflects trader expectations, not guaranteed outcomes
- **YT yield is variable** — Actual yield collected depends on underlying performance
</Callout>
