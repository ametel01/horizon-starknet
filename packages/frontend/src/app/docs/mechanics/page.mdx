## Key Formulas at a Glance

### Price Relationship

<Formula display>{"P_{PT} + P_{YT} = 1 \\text{ SY}"}</Formula>

### Implied APY

<Formula display>{"\\text{APY}_{implied} = \\left( \\frac{1}{P_{PT}} \\right)^{\\frac{365}{d}} - 1"}</Formula>

### PT Price from APY

<Formula display>{"P_{PT} = \\frac{1}{(1 + \\text{APY})^{\\frac{d}{365}}}"}</Formula>

### Minting

<Formula display>{"\\text{PT}_{minted} = \\text{SY}_{deposited} \\times \\text{Index}_{PY}"}</Formula>

### Redemption

<Formula display>{"\\text{SY}_{received} = \\frac{\\text{PT}_{burned}}{\\text{Index}_{PY}}"}</Formula>
