# Getting Started

Make your first deposit in under 5 minutes.

---

## Before You Begin

You'll need:
- A **Starknet wallet** ([Ready](https://argent.xyz) (ex Argent) or [Braavos](https://braavos.app))
- **Test tokens** (get free hrzSTRK from the [Faucet](/faucet))
- Small amount of **STRK for gas**

---

## Simple vs Advanced Mode

Horizon offers two interface modes to match your experience level:

| Mode | Best For | Features |
|------|----------|----------|
| **Simple** (default) | New users | Streamlined deposit/withdraw, automatic yield optimization |
| **Advanced** | Experienced users | PT/YT trading, manual minting, full market controls |

<Callout type="info">
Toggle between modes using the switch in the top navigation bar. Start with Simple Mode—you can switch anytime.
</Callout>

---

## Step 1: Connect Wallet

Click **Connect** in the top right. Select your wallet and approve the connection.

## Step 2: Go to Earn

Click **Earn** in the navigation bar.

## Step 3: Choose a Market

Select based on:
- **Token** you want to deposit
- **APY** offered
- **Maturity** date that fits your timeline

## Step 4: Enter Amount

Type how much you want to deposit. Review the fixed APY shown.

## Step 5: Confirm

Click **Deposit** and approve the transaction in your wallet.

<Callout type="info">
First deposit requires two transactions: one to approve the token, one to deposit.
</Callout>

<div className="mt-6">
  <TryItButton href="/mint">Start Earning Now</TryItButton>
</div>

---

## After Depositing

Your deposit is now split into fixed and variable rate positions. View them anytime on the **Portfolio** page.

**At maturity:** Redeem your position for the original amount plus earned yield.

---

## Common Mistakes

- **Withdrawing early** — May get less than expected
- **Ignoring maturity** — Make sure the date works for you
- **Low gas** — Keep STRK in wallet for transactions
