## TVL Statistics

Total Value Locked (TVL) measures the total assets deposited in the protocol.

      <TableCell>Underlying assets wrapped in SY tokens</TableCell>
    </TableRow>
  </TableBody>
</Table>

### What TVL Indicates

- **Higher TVL** — More liquidity, lower slippage, more stable prices
- **Growing TVL** — Increasing protocol adoption
- **TVL per market** — Indicates which maturities are most popular

<Callout type="info">
TVL is denominated in the underlying asset value, not token counts.
</Callout>

### Interpreting Scores

- **80-100**: Excellent — low slippage, suitable for large trades
- **60-79**: Good — reasonable for medium trades
- **40-59**: Fair — expect some slippage, split large orders
- **Below 40**: Poor — high risk of slippage, trade cautiously

## Advanced Analytics

### Implied vs Realized Yield

Track the spread between market-implied yield and actual underlying yield:

- **Implied > Realized**: Market expects rates to rise
- **Implied < Realized**: Market expects rates to fall
- **Convergence**: Accurate market pricing

- **Cumulative fees**: Total fees generated by a pool
- **Fee APY**: Annualized return for LPs from fees
- **Fee trends**: Rising fees indicate growing activity
