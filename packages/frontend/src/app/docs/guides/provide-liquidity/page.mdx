# Provide Liquidity

Earn trading fees by adding liquidity to PT/SY pools.

---

## When to Use This Strategy

- You want to earn **passive income** from swap fees
- You're comfortable with **impermanent loss** risk
- You plan to hold until **near maturity**

---

## What You Provide

Liquidity pools contain **PT + SY** in a specific ratio.

When you add liquidity:
- Deposit both PT and SY
- Receive **LP tokens** representing your share
- Earn fees from every swap in the pool

---

## Step-by-Step

### 1. Go to Pools

Click **Pools** in the navigation (Advanced mode only).

### 2. Select a Pool

Choose based on:
- **Fee APY** — trading fee returns
- **TVL** — total liquidity (higher = more stable)
- **Maturity** — when the pool expires

### 3. Enter Amounts

Enter how much to deposit. The interface auto-balances to match the pool ratio.

### 4. Confirm

Click **Add Liquidity** and approve in your wallet.

<Callout type="info">
You may need to approve both PT and SY tokens before depositing.
</Callout>

## Removing Liquidity

1. Go to **Pools**
2. Click on your active position
3. Click **Remove Liquidity**
4. Choose amount to withdraw
5. Confirm transaction

You receive PT + SY back in the current pool ratio.

---

## Impermanent Loss

<Callout type="warning">
If implied yield moves significantly in either direction, your LP position may underperform simply holding PT + SY separately.
</Callout>

**IL is lower when:**
- Implied yield stays stable
- You're closer to maturity (PT converges to 1)

**IL is higher when:**
- Large yield swings occur
- Far from maturity

---

## Tips

- Pools near maturity have lower IL risk
- Higher TVL pools have lower price impact for LPs
- Monitor your position regularly on the Portfolio page
