# Earn Fixed Yield

Lock in a guaranteed APY by holding Principal Tokens until maturity.

---

## When to Use This Strategy

- You want **predictable returns**
- You expect rates to **drop** in the future
- You can hold until **maturity**

---

## How It Works

1. Buy PT at a discount (e.g., 0.95 per 1 underlying)
2. Hold until maturity
3. Redeem for full value (1.00)
4. Your profit = the discount

**The discount is your fixed yield, locked in from day one.**

---

## Step-by-Step

### 1. Go to Earn

Click **Earn** in the navigation (Simple mode) or **Mint** (Advanced mode).

### 2. Select a Market

Choose based on:
- **Fixed APY** — higher = better return
- **Maturity** — must fit your timeline
- **Token** — the asset you want exposure to

### 3. Enter Amount

Type how much to deposit. The interface shows:
- Your fixed APY
- Amount of PT you'll receive
- Maturity date

### 4. Confirm Deposit

Click **Deposit** and approve in your wallet.

<Callout type="info">
First-time deposits require two transactions: approve + deposit.
</Callout>

---

## At Maturity

Go to **Portfolio** and click **Redeem**. Your PT converts to the underlying asset at 1:1.

## Risks

<Callout type="warning">
- **Early exit** — Selling PT before maturity may result in losses if rates have risen
- **Opportunity cost** — If rates rise significantly, you miss out on higher yields
</Callout>

---

## Tips

- Choose maturities that match when you'll need the funds
- Longer maturities usually offer higher fixed APY
- Check the current implied yield vs underlying yield to assess value
