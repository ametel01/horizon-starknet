## Claiming Yield

YT accrues yield continuously from the underlying asset.

### To Claim

1. Go to **Portfolio**
2. Find position with claimable yield
3. Click **Claim**
4. Confirm transaction

<Callout type="info">
Yield is paid in the underlying asset (e.g., sSTRK).
</Callout>

---

## Redeeming at Maturity

After maturity, PT can be redeemed 1:1 for the underlying.

### To Redeem PT

1. Go to **Portfolio**
2. Find your matured position
3. Click **Redeem**
4. Confirm transaction

You receive the underlying asset directly to your wallet.

<Callout type="warning">
YT expires worthless at maturity. Claim any accrued yield before expiry.
</Callout>

---

## Early Redemption (Before Maturity)

You can exit early by combining PT + YT back to SY.

### Requirements
- Equal amounts of PT and YT
- Same market/maturity

### To Redeem Early

1. Go to **Portfolio**
2. Click **Redeem** on your position
3. Select early redemption
4. Confirm transaction

You receive SY tokens, which can be unwrapped to the underlying.

---

## Selling Positions

If you don't have matching PT + YT, sell on the market instead:

1. Go to **Trade**
2. Select the token to sell (PT or YT)
3. Swap for SY
4. Unwrap SY to underlying
