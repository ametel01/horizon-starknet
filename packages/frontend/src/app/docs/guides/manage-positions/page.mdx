## Portfolio Summary

At the top of the Portfolio page, you'll see key metrics summarizing your holdings:

- **Total Portfolio Value** - Combined USD value of all your PT, YT, and LP positions
- **Total PnL** - Profit/loss across all positions since acquisition
- **Claimable Yield** - Total yield available to claim across all YT positions
- **Active Positions** - Count of positions with non-zero balances

### Claim All Button

The **Claim All** button lets you collect yield from all YT positions in a single transaction:

1. Click **Claim All** in the portfolio summary
2. Review the total amount to be claimed
3. Confirm the transaction

<Callout type="info">
Claim All batches multiple claims into one transaction, saving gas compared to claiming each position individually.
</Callout>

## Claiming Yield

YT accrues yield continuously from the underlying asset.

### To Claim

1. Go to **Portfolio**
2. Find position with claimable yield
3. Click **Claim**
4. Confirm transaction

<Callout type="info">
Yield is paid in the underlying asset (e.g., sSTRK).
</Callout>

---

## Claiming Rewards

Some YT positions earn external reward tokens in addition to yield. These rewards come from the underlying protocol (e.g., staking rewards, liquidity mining incentives).

<Callout type="info">
Not all YT positions have reward tokens. Rewards depend on the underlying asset and protocol integration.
</Callout>

### Viewing Available Rewards

1. Go to **Portfolio**
2. Find your YT position
3. Check the **Rewards** column for claimable amounts

Positions with no external rewards will show "—" in the rewards column.

### To Claim Rewards

1. Go to **Portfolio**
2. Find position with claimable rewards
3. Click **Claim Rewards**
4. Confirm transaction

Rewards are paid directly in the reward token (e.g., STRK, protocol tokens).

### Combined Yield + Rewards Claim

For gas efficiency, you can claim both yield and rewards in a single transaction:

1. Go to **Portfolio**
2. Click **Claim All** on a position with both yield and rewards
3. Confirm transaction

This batches the yield claim (in underlying asset) and reward claims (in reward tokens) into one transaction.

<Callout type="warning">
Like yield, reward accrual stops at maturity. Claim any rewards before expiry.
</Callout>

## Redeeming at Maturity

After maturity, PT can be redeemed 1:1 for the underlying.

### To Redeem PT

1. Go to **Portfolio**
2. Find your matured position
3. Click **Redeem**
4. Confirm transaction

You receive the underlying asset directly to your wallet.

<Callout type="warning">
YT expires worthless at maturity. Claim any accrued yield before expiry.
</Callout>

---

## Early Redemption (Before Maturity)

You can exit early by combining PT + YT back to SY.

### Requirements
- Equal amounts of PT and YT
- Same market/maturity

### To Redeem Early

1. Go to **Portfolio**
2. Click **Redeem** on your position
3. Select early redemption
4. Confirm transaction

You receive SY tokens, which can be unwrapped to the underlying.

---

## Selling Positions

If you don't have matching PT + YT, sell on the market instead:

1. Go to **Trade**
2. Select the token to sell (PT or YT)
3. Swap for SY
4. Unwrap SY to underlying
