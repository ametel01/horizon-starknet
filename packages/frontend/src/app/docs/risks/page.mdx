# Risks

Understanding the risks involved in using Horizon Protocol.

---

## Smart Contract Risk

<Callout type="danger">
Horizon smart contracts have not been audited. Use at your own risk and never deposit more than you can afford to lose.
</Callout>

**What this means:**
- Code may contain bugs or vulnerabilities
- Funds could be lost due to exploits
- No guarantees of contract behavior

**Mitigation:**
- Start with small amounts
- Monitor protocol announcements
- Understand the code if possible

### Liquidity Risk

- Low liquidity pools have high price impact
- Large positions may be difficult to exit
- Slippage can significantly reduce returns

## Underlying Asset Risks

### Depeg Risk

If the underlying yield-bearing asset loses its peg or value:
- SY value drops proportionally
- PT redemption value may be affected
- YT yield distributions decrease

### Protocol Risk

The underlying protocol (e.g., Nostra for sSTRK) has its own risks:
- Smart contract vulnerabilities
- Oracle failures
- Governance attacks

---

## Position-Specific Risks

### PT Risks

<Callout type="warning">
**Early exit risk** — If you sell PT before maturity and implied yields have risen, you will realize a loss.
</Callout>

- **Opportunity cost** — Locked into a fixed rate while market rates may rise
- **Maturity mismatch** — May need funds before maturity date

See [Earn Fixed Yield guide](/docs/guides/earn-fixed-yield) for PT strategies.

### YT Risks

<Callout type="danger">
**YT expires worthless** — At maturity, YT has zero value. All unclaimed yield is lost if not collected.
</Callout>

- **High volatility** — Small yield changes cause large price swings
- **Time decay** — Value decreases as maturity approaches
- **Variable returns** — Actual yield may differ from expectations

See [Trade Yield guide](/docs/guides/trade-yield) for YT strategies.

### LP Risks

- **Impermanent loss** — Position underperforms holding assets separately
- **Complexity** — Multiple sources of returns and risks
- **Market maker risk** — LPs absorb directional flow

See [Provide Liquidity guide](/docs/guides/provide-liquidity) for LP strategies.

### Reward Token Risks

Some yield-bearing assets distribute external reward tokens in addition to their base yield. Not all markets on Horizon have reward tokens — this section applies only to positions that do.

<Callout type="warning">
**Reward rates can change** — External reward programs may be reduced, paused, or discontinued by the underlying protocol at any time.
</Callout>

- **Reward rate volatility** — APR from rewards fluctuates based on emissions schedule, token price, and total deposits
- **Protocol dependency** — Rewards depend on the external protocol's continued operation and incentive programs
- **Smart contract risk** — Reward distribution contracts are separate from the yield source and carry their own risks
- **Claiming requirements** — Rewards must be claimed before YT expiry; unclaimed rewards may be forfeited

**Before relying on reward yields:**
- Verify the reward program is active and check its duration
- Understand that projected reward APR is an estimate, not a guarantee
- Factor in potential reward token price volatility

## Operational Risks

### Wallet Security

- **Private key loss** — No recovery possible
- **Phishing** — Always verify URLs and contract addresses
- **Malicious approvals** — Review all transaction details

### Network Risks

- **Transaction failures** — Gas estimation or network congestion
- **Reorg risk** — Wait for confirmations on large transactions
- **RPC issues** — Consider using multiple RPC endpoints

---

## Risk Management Tips

1. **Diversify** — Don't put all funds in one position or market
2. **Size appropriately** — Only risk what you can afford to lose
3. **Understand before acting** — Read docs, ask questions
4. **Monitor positions** — Check regularly, especially near maturity
5. **Set reminders** — Calendar alerts for maturity dates and yield claims
